About Excellence Academy

Discover our rich history, unwavering mission, and the dedicated team that makes Excellence Academy a beacon of educational excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 1990, Excellence Academy began as a small community school with a big vision: to provide world-class education that nurtures not just academic excellence, but also character, creativity, and critical thinking.

Over the past three decades, we have grown from a modest institution serving 50 students to a thriving educational community of over 1,200 students from diverse backgrounds, all united by our shared commitment to learning and growth.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, from pioneering technology integration in classrooms to developing comprehensive programs that address the whole child - academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

Our Journey Through Time

1990

Foundation

Excellence Academy was founded with 50 students and 8 dedicated teachers, establishing our commitment to educational excellence.

1995

First Expansion

Added secondary education program and expanded facilities to accommodate growing student body of 200 students.
