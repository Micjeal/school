Frequently Asked Questions

What are your admission requirements?

+

Our admission requirements include completed application form, academic transcripts, recommendation letters, personal statement, and an assessment interview. Specific requirements may vary by grade level.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

+

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, sibling discounts, and flexible payment plans. Contact our admissions office to discuss available options.

Can I schedule a campus tour?

+

Absolutely! We offer guided campus tours Monday through Friday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, and Saturday at 10:00 AM. Please call or email to schedule your visit.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

+

We offer a wide range of activities including sports teams, music and drama clubs, debate society, science clubs, community service groups, and various academic competitions.
