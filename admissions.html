Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form along with required documents including academic transcripts, recommendation letters, and personal statement.

2

Assessment & Interview

Participate in our assessment process which includes academic evaluation and a personal interview with our admissions team and academic staff.

3

School Visit

Visit our campus for a guided tour, meet with teachers and current students, and experience our learning environment firsthand.

4

Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision within 2-3 weeks. Successful applicants will receive enrollment information and next steps.

5

Enrollment

Complete enrollment by submitting required forms, paying fees, and attending orientation sessions before the academic year begins.
